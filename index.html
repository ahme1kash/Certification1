                      Game Rules 

         Rock beats scissors, scissors beat 
         paper, and paper beats rock.

         count off “rock, paper, scissors,
         shoot” or just “rock, paper, scissors.”

            
         Use rock, paper, scissors to settle
         minor decisions or simply play to
         pass the time
